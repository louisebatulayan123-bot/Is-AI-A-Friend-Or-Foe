Is AI a friend, a foe, or something between?

01

Disadvantages of AI in School

  • Can make cheating easier! Some students might copy answers from AI instead of trying the work themselves, which is unfair on other students.

  • Not always correct! AI can sometimes give wrong or outdated information, so you still need to check with a teacher or trusted source.

  • Less practice for real skills! If you let AI do all the writing, thinking, or maths, it can be harder to build your own skills for tests and exams.

02

Disadvantages of AI in Your Everyday Life

  • Less privacy! Some AI systems collect data about what you search, watch, or click on, which can feel like you are being constantly watched and tracked online.

  • Too much screen time! Smart recommendations can keep you scrolling or watching for longer than you planned.

  • Can spread bias! If the data used to train AI is unfair, the AI can repeat those unfair patterns in things like adverts or recommendations.

03

So, How can we use AI safely?

  • Use AI as a helper, not a replacement! Let it give ideas or explain things, but still think for yourself and do your own work.

  • Check important information! It might be tempting to use AI, however, for school work or big decisions, always double‑check AI answers with teachers, trusted websites, or books.

  • Protect your privacy! Avoid sharing personal details with AI tools and talk to an adult if you see something online that feels unsafe or worrying.
