Benefits of AI in School

  • Helps with homework! AI tools like ChatGPT can quickly answer questions, explain difficult topics differently, and suggest ideas for projects.

  • Makes learning fun! AI can turn lessons into games or interactive challenges, which can help students stay interested and motivated when learning.

  • Learning at your own pace! With AI, you can practice anytime, whether at home or school, learning faster or spending more time on harder topics.

Benefits of AI in Your Everyday Life

  • Saves time! AI helps with everyday tasks such as checking the weather, finding routes when travelling, or reminding you of events or homework deadlines.

  • Keeps you organised! Virtual assistants (like Siri or Alexa) can set you reminders, alarms, or help you manage yours or parents' schedule!

  • Accessibility! AI tools can read text aloud or help people with learning differences understand information more easily.
